Features

Everything your logbook does.
Nothing it can't.

One app replaces the binders, clipboards, and spreadsheets.

Cycle Logging

Two-phase load/unload workflow. Record machine, batch, cycle type, instrument count, timing, and result — with separate staff sign-off at each step.

Spore Test Tracking

Log placement, incubation, and results. Auto-determines pass/fail from control and test strip outcomes.

Maintenance Logs

Track cleaning, drain cycles, filter changes, gasket replacements, and calibrations. Set due dates so nothing slips.

PDF Reports

Generate landscape reports for any date range. Share via email, print, or messaging — ready for audits in seconds.

Staff & Machine Management

Register sterilizers and staff once. Both populate dropdown selectors across the app for fast, consistent entry.

iCloud Backup

Auto-syncs to your private iCloud storage. Data persists across reinstalls and device changes. No accounts to create, no fees.

How It Works

Mirrors your real workflow

Load, run, unload, verify — SteriVault follows the same sequence your team already uses.

Load the Cycle

Pick the machine, enter the batch number, choose pouches or wrapped, count instruments, and tap to log. The cycle is saved and marked in progress.

Unload & Verify

A separate staff member records the unload — time, handler, pass/fail, and any notes. Dual accountability, built in.

Report & Comply

Pull a date-range PDF anytime. Every data point is captured and formatted — ready for audits or internal review.
